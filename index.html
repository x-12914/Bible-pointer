Voice Commands Cheat Sheet

  • Standard Verse: "John three sixteen" or "John 3:16"
  • Single Chapter: "Psalm twenty-three" or "Psalm 23"
  • Verse Ranges: "Genesis one verses one to five" or "Romans 8:28-30"
  • Multi-word Books: "First Corinthians fifteen fifty-five" or "1 John 1:9"
